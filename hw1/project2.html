Description

YardLoop is a neighborhood-focused marketplace that connects local sellers hosting yard/garage sales with buyers in nearby communities. It aggregates yard sale events, items for sale, photos, and real-time messaging so neighbors can discover sales, ask sellers questions, save favorites, and plan visits. The app blends map-based discovery, item-level listings, and lightweight social features like check-ins and conversations.
